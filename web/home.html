Latest Updates

St. Andrew's URC, Cardiff is now open for weekly Sunday services, hosted from 11am to 12pm. No booking or reservation is required to attend. You will be required to wear a mask upon entry and when moving around the church, as well as providing track and trace information and passing a temperature check. Please do not attend if you are feeling unwell, have a high temperature or a persistent cough.
