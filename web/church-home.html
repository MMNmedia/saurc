Latest Information: We are OPEN!

Our church is open as usual for weekly services and for the organisations that use our church facilities. However, we are following the latest COVID-19 guidelines, and services, events or hiring sessions may change or be cancelled without notice.

Yes! Our church is open as usual for weekly church services, monthly communion, the Coffee Court, and the organisations that use our church on a regular basis.

Yes, we have our own track and trace forms, as well as a QR code that can be scanned with the NHS COVID-19 app.

Wearing a mask or face covering indoors is mandatory, apart from when you are sitting in Coffee Court areas. If you are medically exempt (or have another valid exemption from wearing a face covering), we kindly ask that you bring proof of exemption. Social distancing is encouraged and will be enforced depending on local guidelines. Hand sanitiser is available throughout the church and Coffee Court. Please do not attend if you are feeling unwell, have a high temperature or a persistent cough. Every other pew is closed off for social distancing. Toilet facilities are only available to church members and paying Coffee Court customers, and may not be open for your visit.

Whether you haven't been to church in a while, or if you haven't worshipped with us before, you are more than welcome to our weekly Sunday services, which typically start at 11am and end at 12pm. No registration necessary!

No bookings or reservations are required for any church services. Feel free to just turn up! Note: this does not apply to ticketed events.

At time of writing, our church is exempt from operating a COVID Pass scheme (due to capacity limits). With that said, please wear face coverings and bring a valid COVID Pass just in case. Unfortunately, vaccination cards cannot be accepted, as per Welsh Government advice. Make sure you keep up to date with the event organiser to check for any restrictions or updates to the event.

Unfortunately, we cannot issue refunds or directly assist with ticketing issues. Please contact the event organiser for more information. You are welcome to contact Matthew Tyler-Howells (matthew@matthewsnetmedia.co.uk) if you require assistance with obtaining a refund from the event organiser.

Our church facilities are open to all organisations who hire out our church spaces. However, it is your responsibility to check with your organisation leader as to whether meetings are going ahead or not.
